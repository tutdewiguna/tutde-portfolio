A Journey of Property Branding Video Shooting in Lovina

September 18, 2025 | by Tut De Satria Wiguna

Recently I had an incredible opportunity to do a property branding video shoot at New Sunari Lovina Beach Resort Lovina Bali. This project became one of the most memorable work experiences in my career not only because of the stunning location but also because I was joined by my full supportive team.

The journey started in the morning. There were five of us, myself, Andika, Ratih, Ria, and Pak Gusde our driver who also kept us entertained along the way. During the drive to Lovina we shared laughter stories and discussed creative ideas for how to capture the best shots for the project.

Once we arrived at the resort we were amazed by its calm beachfront atmosphere fresh air and the warm hospitality of the staff. The place gave off both a professional and relaxing vibe making it perfect for our shoot.

A Smooth and Enjoyable Shooting Process

Working with a full team creates a positive energy that makes every project easier and more fun.

This shoot felt different because we had our full team present. Positive energy could be felt from start to finish. We began by filming the resort’s stunning exterior views followed by the interior areas such as the rooms lobby and restaurant. Even though the schedule was tight we hardly felt tired because of the fun and supportive teamwork.

In the evening after shooting wrapped up we went out to eat together around Lovina. This was an important moment for us not only to recharge but also to bond and share laughter. These moments off set became unforgettable memories.

The next morning we enjoyed breakfast at the resort’s restaurant. The variety of food the calm seaside atmosphere and the fresh morning air made us feel even more energized. After breakfast we continued with our final shooting session until everything was complete.

Memories and Lessons to Take Home

After wrapping up the project we headed back to the office with satisfying footage. But more valuable than the results were the teamwork experiences and lessons we gained. I realized that working with a solid team makes even demanding tasks feel lighter and every project can become a memorable bonding experience.
